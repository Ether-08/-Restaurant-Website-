About us

At Italiano, we bring the soul of Italy to your table through time-honored recipes and a passion for authentic flavors. Nestled within a warm, rustic atmosphere of exposed brick and soft amber light, our kitchen specializes in handmade pastas, stone-fired pizzas, and carefully curated wines.

We believe that a great meal is more than just food—it’s an opportunity to slow down and share a moment with the people you love. Whether you’re joining us for a celebratory feast or a quiet glass of Chianti, you’ll always find a seat at our family table. Benvenuti!

Whether you’re sharing a romantic evening, celebrating a milestone, or simply enjoying a glass of Chianti after a long day, you are always famiglia here.
